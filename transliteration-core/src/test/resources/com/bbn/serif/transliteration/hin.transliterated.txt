kailiphorniyaa (/ˌkaeliˈfornje/) samyukta raajya amerikaa ke pashcimii tatt par sthit ek raajya hai. yah amerikaa kaa sabase adhik aabaadii aur kssetraphal mem alaaskaa aur tteksas ke pashcaat tiisaraa sabase baddaa raajya haim. kailiphorniyaa ke uupar aurigan, aur usake niice meksiko hai. kailiphorniyaa kii raajadhaanii saikraamenntto hai. samyukta raajya amerikaa kaa aadhaa phal is raajya se aataa hai.[1] agar kailiphorniyaa ek svatamtra desh hotaa to duniyaa mem usakaa sakal ghareluu utpaad (gii. ddii. pii.) 8 yaa 9 sthaan par hotaa aur janasamkhyaa 34ve sthaan par hotii.[2]
vyutpatti[sampaadit karem]
kailiphorniyaa shabda kaa pahalaa artha thaa jo kssetra jahaam aaj baahaa kailiphorniyaa praayadviip, nevaaddaa, yuuttaa aur erijonaa, nayaa meksiko, aur vaayomimga ke kaii vibhaag sthit haim.
janasaamkhyikii[sampaadit karem]
1 julaaii 2013 ko kailiphorniyaa kaa janasamkhyaa 38,332,521 thaa. yah 2010 se 2.9% adhik hai.[3]2000 aur 2009 ke biic mem, janasamkhyaa 3,090,016 se baddhii. (5,058,440 janma ghattaa 2,179,958 maut).[4] 1990 se lagabhag 3.4 laakh kailiphorniyaa ke log anya raajyom ko cale gae, jais ki tteksas, nevaaddaa aur erijonaa.[5]
